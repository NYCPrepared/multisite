New filters, wp_biographia_content_title and wp_biographia_link_item, to override the Biography Box title and each element of a contact link.

New template tags, wpb_get_biography_box and wpb_the_biography_box.

Contact link URLs in a user's profile are now checked and validated.

The color of the Biography Box border can now be specified.

Support for per post overrides to assist sites with guest authors.

Prevent the Biography Box from showing up in the sidebar or footer due to other plugins use of the_content or the_excerpt.
